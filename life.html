Student Life

We encourage our students to be excellent learners with a growth mindset! Our students excel at

Athletics

We promote a healthy lifestyle for our students by providing them with athletic opportunities. Our basketball and soccer teams are a great way for students to socialize and have fun! We also ensure that students have time during the school day to exercise to enhance their learning.

Clubs

We believe in creating well rounded students. In order to do this, we encourage them to explore their interests outside of academics by participating in clubs. Our organized clubs include art club, cooking club, choir club, and many more.

Events

In order to instill school spirit in our students and keep morale high, we frequently organize exciting events for students and their families. We regularly hold movie nights, readathons, bookfairs, and even school parties! Check our school calendar to view upcoming events and RSVP.
